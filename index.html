Esther Hwang

About Me

Esther Hwang is an illustrator and designer based in snowy Minnesota. She graduated with a BFA in Animation from the Minneapolis College of Art and Design and has since worked on Amazon Prime's Danger and Eggs as well as an upcoming Netflix show Twelve Forever. Her work often includes whimsical characters looking for their next adventure.


She can often be found raoming second hand bookshops, sipping Chai tea, or writing comics that may or may not see the light of day.
